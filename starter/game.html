Rule of the game

the pig dice game is a fun turn-based game. Here are the rules

  1. Each turn, a player rolls a dice as many time as they wish
  2. The dice value are added to their current score
  3. if the player roles 1 their current score is lost, and the turn ends
  4. The player can choose to 'Hold' to add their current score to teir total score
  5. The first player to reach 100 wins the game
